Frontier AI's Impact on Cybersecurity Workshop


Recent advances in large-scale AI systems are rapidly reshaping both offensive and defensive cybersecurity. Recent AI-in-cybersecurity benchmarks (CyberGym and BountyBench), along with the recent AIxCC’s results, demonstrate that AI can meaningfully support real-world cybersecurity tasks, including zero-day discovery. While these advances highlight AI’s potential to strengthen defenses, their impact on offensive uses also appears to be increasing more and more, as outlined in the recent SoK paper. Accurately assessing these offensive and defensive capabilities is therefore essential.

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners across AI and security to assess the current landscape of AI-enabled cyber threats and defenses, explore future risk prediction, and identify opportunities for collaboration. By fostering this dialogue, we aim to help the community prepare proactively for the era of frontier AI–augmented cybersecurity. The workshop will feature presentations from winning teams of AIxCC, leading researchers from frontier AI labs, academia, and major technology and security companies. Please join us at this pivotal moment for AI in cybersecurity and share your insights with the community!
